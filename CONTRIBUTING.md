# Contributing to Abstractive Machines Projects

Thank you for your interest in Abstractive Machines projects.

## Contribution Policy

All projects under this organization are developed and maintained by Abstractive Machines LLC. **External contributions are not accepted at this time.** All code in these repositories is authored by Abstractive Machines LLC to maintain clear intellectual property ownership.

If you have found a bug or have a feature suggestion, please open an issue in the relevant repository. We appreciate feedback and will consider it for future development.

## Questions

For questions about this policy or any project, open an issue in the relevant repository or contact the maintainer [@nullsync](https://github.com/nullsync).
